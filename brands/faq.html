Frequently Asked


Learn what you can do as an ARBOOX Brand.

 





What is ARBOOX for Brands?


ARBOOX for Brands is a programme that allows businesses of all sizes to use tools developed by ARBOOX to increase awareness, sales and content views. Brands can also sell merchandise and create AR experiences.


How do I sign up?


To become an ARBOOX Brand, you need to create a brand profile. Just tap the 'Get started' button located in the topbar of this page. You will be redirected to our profile creator. Once in the profile creator, you will be asked to provide a few details about your brand and upload a header image.


What's the cost of being an ARBOOX Brand?


There is no cost. The only service we'll make money from is advertising.


Are there any review processes?


Yes. At first, we will review your brand profile before making it public in the ARBOOX app. There is also a review process to ensure the merchandise you sell in the app is authentic. Both review process are simple, but it can take up to 24 hours to review your request and publish your latest changes to ARBOOX.


Can I personalise my brand profile?


Yes. Your brand profile is fully personalisable. You can set up a custom header image, add a brief description, and a website. The more content you upload to ARBOOX (merchandise, stories, videos and AR experiences), the more options you get to personalise your profile.


What is ARBOOX Studio?


ARBOOX Studio is our AR experience production software, but it's also were you can manage your brand and the place to create advertisements. From ARBOOX Studio you can upload new products and videos, edit your brand profile, and get monthly insights. Learn more at arboox.com/studio.


How can I opt-in to make my products visible in AR?


When uploading a product in ARBOOX Studio, you will have the choice to provide a 3D model to make your products previewable in augmented reality.


My brand does not sell any goods, can I still sign up?


Of course. Even if you don't have merchandise to sell, you can still take advantage of our tools to share your content.


Are paid partnerships allowed?


Yes. Paid partnerships are permitted as longs as the content being promoted does not make reference to mature content.


What about creators?


Although the programme is called ARBOOX for Brands, we do accept content creators. We offer all the tools required to grow a community and build a following.


More questions?


If you have any other questions regarding the programme, we'll be happy to help. Please contact us via e-mail. We're available from Monday to Friday, from 9 AM to 6 PM CST (Central Standard Time).
