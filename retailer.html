ARBOOX™





Features


With ARBOOX™, you can view AR experiences and instantly launch them within your device. The app comes packed with multiple features like a world map, dark interface, and even, a TV streaming service. ARBOOX™ is 100% free.


AR Experiences
ARBOOX™ receives an update every week, adding new AR experience to the app. Sometimes, you'll be able to try Instagram and Snapchat filters.


TV Shows
ARBOOX™ pulls popular videos from YouTube and lets you view them within the app.


Dark Mode
A dramatic dark interface is included as well to help you use the app at night.


World Map
There's a world map inside the app that lets people locate in-place AR experiences around.


Stories and Bites
ARBOOX™ lets users read articles from lots of websites. Bites are mini games you can play inside the app.
