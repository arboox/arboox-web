Type Museum


In collaboration with Pangram Pangram® Foundry, we built Type Museum: a place to watch some of the most beloved typefaces in action, all in augmented reality.



What you'll find


We've crafted Type Museum with love, and every thing on it is super high quality. There are five typefaces you can spot on this AR museum: Editorial New, Mondwest, NeueBit, Monument Extended and Neue Machina.We've also included some decoration (houseplants and palm trees), so you have an amazing experience.

Gestures


There are some gestures you can use to control objects in AR:

  • Drag and drop the object with one finger to change its position.

  • Pinch with two fingers to zoom in or zoom out. Works for rescaling the objects in the AR experience.

  • Pan the object with two fingers to rotate it.
