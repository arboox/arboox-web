Experience cards


Just tap on a card to open an augmented reality Experience. New cards are added every week.





Built-in Games


You can play bite-sized games right from the ARBOOX™ app. All games come with low-data consumption by default. To find the game library, tap the   agriculture   icon.





Watch videos


Every week, the video tab is updated and it shows three popular and/or interesting videos. To open the video screen, tap the   play_circle   icon.





Shop in augmented reality


Although this is a beta feature, some shops who sell products in ARBOOX™ let you preview their products in augmented reality. To unlock this section of the app, tap the   shopping_bag   icon.





Read stories about your favourite topics


Story cards will ocassionally appear in the home section. Tap one to read about the topic.
