We're proudly a mexican startup. Our mission is to make augmented reality accessible to anyone, that's why we started our worldwide expansion with countries like Australia, Canada, United Kingdom, Ireland, USA, France, Saudi Arabia, UAE, Qatar, Egypt, Norway, Sweden, Finland, and more to come soon.


We put people at the center of everything we do


ARBOOX™ is an augmented reality app made for everyone. This means that accessibility is present at all times, from the design to the development and the production. We always think about our users' needs to shape the look and feel of the app.



Around the globe we go


At ARBOOX™, we're open to meeting new people, new cultures and living the experiences. That's why, we've set the goal to open physical stores and galleries by 2025 worldwide. Our first store will be located at the heart of Nuevo Polanco, Mexico City.



This ain't the store, but it's the zone.


Our future plans


Even though we're just getting started, we already have future plans for ARBOOX™. We announce them each year at the ABWK (ARBOOX™ Worldwide Keynote). For example, we already have some ideas about our HQ, however, you'll hear all about it on January 22nd, 2022.


January 22nd, 2021 is ARBOOX™'s launch date, that's why we make our global conferences as close as we can to that date every year.
