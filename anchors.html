ARBOOX™ has a map that shows you where Anchors are located around the globe. This map can also give you accurate directions to get to that place.






Just scan and launch.


When you arrive at the place where the Anchor is located, just open your camera and scan look for the code in the poster, then scan it. You don't need to have ARBOOX™ installed or to create an account.
